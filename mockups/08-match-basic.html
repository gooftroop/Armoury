Army Rules
Oath of Moment
At the start of your Command phase, select one enemy unit. Until the start of your next Command phase, each time a model from your army makes an attack that targets that enemy unit, you can re-roll the Hit roll and you can re-roll the Wound roll.
Combat Doctrines
During the battle, your army benefits from the active Combat Doctrine. At the start of each battle round, you can change the active doctrine.
